Emma Fordham

Home | About | Artwork | Events | Contact |

Born in the UK in 1974, Emma Fordham has lived in Sweden, London, Oxford and Shanghai before moving to Hong Kong in 2013. After a two year art foundation, training in classical art techniques such as etching, painting, drawing and sculpture she moved to London to continue her studies at The Slade School of Fine Art. She has since shown her work in group and solo exhibitions in both Europe and Asia. Emma Fordham is also a qualified Teacher of Art and Design.
